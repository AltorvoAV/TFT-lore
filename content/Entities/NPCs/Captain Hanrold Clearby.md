**Quick Info:**
- Race: #Gnome 
- Age: ~17
- Occupation: Smugger, & Captain
- Met: [[Brugrove - City of the Iron Guard|Brugrove]]
- Status: #Dead 

A cheery Gnome, [[Captain Hanrold Clearby|Captain Clearby]], ferried the party across the waters towards [[The Isle of Black]], and back. On returning to [[Brugrove - City of the Iron Guard|Brugrove]], [[Captain Hanrold Clearby|Hanrold Clearby]] attempted to sell [[Skapa]] for a few silver, whilst Skapa was hidden in a barrel of fish.

Given the quest by the [[Player Characters|Moonlit Syndicate]] to deliver news to [[King Sedulus II]] and gold to [[Ron Henzit]] (Harbour Master in [[Pirate Bay]]), the party found [[Captain Hanrold Clearby|Captain Clearby]] drunkenly talking to Ron before passing out.

Drachen they commanded a brute (member of [[The Black Gauntlets]]) to step on Hanrold... They crushed his neck killing him instantly, and throwing his lifeless body into the ruthless waves lapping against [[Pirate Bay]]'s docks.
