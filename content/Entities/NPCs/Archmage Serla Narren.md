A talented mage from [[Porthca - The Arcane Bridge|Porthca]] who ventured North East to explore [[Brugrove - City of the Iron Guard|Brugrove]], [[Valonde - The Pearled Coast|Valonde]], and other cities as part of [[The Sunrise Establishment (WWFtM)]]. During his time, he gained great knowledge, riches, and fame... far from where he came from.

Having studied at the [[College of Porthca]] in the art of adventuring, [[Archmage Serla Narren|Serla]] rose to the great heights of Archmage.

We now follow his journey across [[Nyropa]] into the [[Shazahath Mountains - The Ruined Caverns|Shazahath Mountains]] with [[The Sunrise Establishment (WWFtM)]].
