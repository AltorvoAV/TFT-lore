[[Dr Zagak Iscara|Dr Iscara]], was the beloved [[Dr Zagak Iscara|Royal Inventor]] for [[Brugrove - City of the Iron Guard|Brugrove]]; a trusted advisor to [[King Sedulus II|The King]].

The [[Player Characters|Moonlit Syndicate]] were sent to investigate [[Dr Zagak Iscara|Dr Iscara]]'s disappearance, where it turned out he seemed to have gone mad, his family dead, and some sort of incantation brewing... the [[Player Characters|Moonlit Syndicate]] destroyed Iscara, leaving nothing left but his necklace.

When the party found [[Dr Zagak Iscara|Doctor Iscara]], he had already been twisted and looked so.
