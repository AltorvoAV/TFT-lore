A twisted contortion of the golden brilliance [[Korraeth, Voice of the Flame|Korraeth]] once bestowed upon [[Nyropa]]. Twisted by the evil convictions of [[She Who Weaves the Vein]].

Before his divulsion to primal instincts, when [[Korraeth, Voice of the Flame|Korraeth]] was the necessary force of harmony between [[Koboldkind]], the Mountains inhabitants, and his own peace; [[Korraeth, Voice of the Flame|Korraeth]] spent the majority of that time in extended hibernation. His breath kept the mountain’s interior warm enough for life: softening ore, heating stone, and maintaining the internal cycles that countless tribes relied upon for survival.

To those who lived upon the slopes and in the tunnels, he was not a mystery—he was a constant.

He was known by titles, not a name:
- [[Korraeth, Voice of the Flame|The Great Flame]]
- [[Korraeth, Voice of the Flame|The Heat Beneath]]
- [[Korraeth, Voice of the Flame|The Ancient Breath]]

Every tribe understood he was a dragon, but none knew his age, lineage, or origins. He had always been there in their memory, and so they assumed he always would be.

He was not a guardian by intention, nor a terror by choice.

He was simply **the mountain’s pulse**.
