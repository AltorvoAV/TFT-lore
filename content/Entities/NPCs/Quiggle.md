**Quick Info:**
- Race: #Halfling 
- Age: 42
- Occupation: Teacher (though was fired because she cannot read)
- Met: On the road to [[The Lost Lair of Souls - The Feted Fortress|The Feted Fortress]]
- Status: #Alive (somehow)

The party met [[Quiggle]] as she was making her way across the road towards [[Brugrove - City of the Iron Guard|Brugrove]], she mentioned how she was travelling to become a teacher (to which [[Skapa]] mentioned a recent opening). She further talked about [[The Lost Lair of Souls - The Feted Fortress|The Feted Fortress]] and how it is believed to be ruled by an immortal being with thousands of undead swarming the location in addition to dragons!

Quiggle's father ([[Quoggle]]) was killed by a teacher, so [[Quiggle]] became a teacher to avenge her father 

Moments, prior to meeting the party, she was robbed of all her 'money', so she gave them her 'Ma Knee' (her only child, who she affectionately called due to the size of them reaching [[Quiggle]]'s knee).

[[Quiggle]] believes deeply in [[Yondalla]], though this would be hard to tell, because she rarely mentions the god at all.
