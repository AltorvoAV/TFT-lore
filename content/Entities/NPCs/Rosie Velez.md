Friend of [[Alyssia Lockraven]], [[Aron Elliot]], [[Aleena Baxter]], & [[Kitty Hess]], [[Rosie Velez|Rosie]] is a friendly gnome smuggler who makes a living moving goods of interest through Eastern [[Nyropa]].

Recently, returned to the [[The Lost City - City of Thieves|Lost City]] after being brutally attacked by masked figures during a high risk, high reward job. Having stolen from 5 of the 7 guilds, [[Rosie Velez|Rosie]] has an incredibly high reward on her head.
