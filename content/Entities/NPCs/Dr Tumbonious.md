**Quick Info:**
- Race: #Tiefling 
- Age: 53
- Occupation: Dr and creator of many magical ailments.
- Met: Fought alongside [[Drachen Lokeriyan|Drachen]] and [[Ayanna Everstride]] in the Fighting Pits of [[Valonde - The Pearled Coast|Valonde]]
- Status: #Alive (as I write this)

As per the commentators speak, [[Dr Tumbonious]] is known for his wonderous healing hands, his considerable weekly donations to the local Orphanage, and by long track record as a well respected and loved member of our community.

The commentator also mentioned how the Dr had risen from the depths of Generational Poverty.
