[[Nester Tarrenshade]], leader of [[The Black Gauntlets]], the enforcers guild of [[The Lost City - City of Thieves|The Lost City]], residing in his district: [[Charnel Steps]].

A giant hulking human known as one of the greatest boxers, and a ruthless gang leader.

A particular distaste, and respect for [[Skapa]] due to an unfortunate botched theft attempt from Skapa.

[[Nester Tarrenshade|Nester]] sits as one of the members of [[The Black Hand]], and one of the executing groups within the [[The Lost City - City of Thieves|City of Thieves]].
