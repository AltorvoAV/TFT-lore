[[Gurk]] the friendly, FAT, and chaotic Hobgoblin, (best) friend of [[Skapa]].

The party met [[Gurk]] in [[The Lost City - City of Thieves|The Lost City]], [[Gallows Market]].

Gurk funs a very successful shop selling a myriad of ~~stolen trash~~ authentic goods! He's a LARGER than life character with an amazing ability to sell just about anything.

Being the kind soul he is; has helped [[Skapa]] many times, from cutting his arm off, to fixing Skapa's punctured lung... due to his steady *'hands of a hanged donkey'*.
