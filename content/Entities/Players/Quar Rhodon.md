Quar Rhodon was once a devout follower of [[Selûne]] however after so many unanswered prayers, too many injustices left unpunished, his beliefs began to waver and in a moment of doubt he heard one voice. "Hope is a lie." Working as a blacksmith [[Quar Rhodon|Rhodon]] is stoic yet approachable even friendly to those around him. Yet, when [[Shar|The Lady of Loss]] calls, he is a butcher; a weapon guided by [[Shar]] to fulfil her goals by any means.

Secretly supplying weapons and armour to [[Shar|Sharran]]s for when the time comes
