[[Aldric Vane|Aldric]] works in a lumber mill ([[Hardstone's Hardwood]]) in [[Grimtide Gate]]. He saved the life of [[Bamik Hardstone]] when Aldric found him unconscious in the surrounding woods.

[[Aldric Vane|Aldric]] also made friends with multiple generous and kindhearted folk in [[The Ruins of Heathorp Keep|Heathorp Keep]]. Most notably, [[Elrond Vetillon|Elrond]], & [[Sargrea Vetillon]], who own [[Selûne's Silks]], in [[South Docks]].

During the [[The Onslaught of Shar (TOS)|The Onslaught of Shar]], [[Aldric Vane|Aldric]] killed [[Elrond Vetillon|Elrond]] in [[South Docks]]. Once the [[Well of Damnation]] ritual was complete, [[Shar]] granted Aldric his secret (that Shar may command his death).
