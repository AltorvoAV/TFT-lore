A 20 year old company based on the outskirts of [[Valonde - The Pearled Coast|Valonde]], specialisation in fine woodcraft, including; instruments, clocks, music boxes...etc.

The company has been known to quietly donate to widows, veterans, & temples of [[Garl Glittergold]].

~10 years ago, the company started offering artisan jewellery in addition to their specialist collection.
