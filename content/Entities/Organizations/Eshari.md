A group of druids who use their magic to accelerate the growth of hardwoods, which in turn makes its own stronger material often known as; Artisanal Hardwood, or Ironwood.

This material is often used by artisans for fine woodcraft, like instruments and other harmless practices. In return, a small group of Valonde guards are posted to ensure the safety of its people and location stay secret.

Only one member of the order was cast out many years back.
