# The Black Hand
A name given to the ruling hand of [[The Lost City - City of Thieves|The Lost City]], due to the city being ran by a [[Kleptocracy]].

The group is made up of the leaders of the 7 guilds:
1. Assassins Guild - [[The Silken Noose]] - [[Orven Veyl]] & [[Myra Veyl]].
2. Enforcers Guild - [[The Black Gauntlets]] - [[Nester Tarrenshade]].
3. Con-Artists Guild - [[Madame Viatrix]].
4. Smugglers Guild - [[Captain G.C.|Captain G-Sea]]
5. Pirates Guild - [[Captain G.C.|Captain Jeasee]]
6. Thieves Guild - [[Little Lark]].
7. Fences Guild - [[Sebastian Hetfield]].
