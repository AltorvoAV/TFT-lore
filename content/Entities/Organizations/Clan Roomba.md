Clan Roomba is a small kobold community living inside the [[Shazahath Mountains - The Ruined Caverns|Shazahath Mountains]].

They’re not malicious — just chaotic, distractible, and deeply superstitious.

They mine a surprisingly pure iron vein beneath the village and trade it with [[Brugrove - City of the Iron Guard|Brugrove]] in exchange for anything shiny, strange, or stick-shaped.

They are known for three things:

1. **Their “[[Chief Roomba]]” who nobody has ever seen.**
2. **Their obsession with metal and “satisfying sticks.”**
3. **Their terrified reverence for [[Korraeth, Voice of the Flame|The Great Flame]]_.**
