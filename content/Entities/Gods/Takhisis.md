## History

Seeds of the Celestial Conflict began at the dawn of time. [[AO]], in their divine balance, creator of all gods, birthed many good, and evil entities. Some yearned for control, utter destruction, or in the worst case would stop at nothing to wield ultimate power. [[Takhisis]] was this worst case. A living embodiment of control, power, ambition, and chaos, who would deceive and manipulate all others in her way in order to become [[The One True God]], [[Asgorath]] (Dragon God of all).

[[Takhisis]] grew her power, enlisting multiple gods and powerful mortals to her side, promising them endless power and control, only to step on their spines, believing herself superior. Through her kaleidoscopic vision, she tore through kin, her rivals, her prey. The [[Astral Plane-Sea]] was littered with dead gods, drifting through the Silver Void.
In the [[Material Plane]], [[Takhisis]] had many fanatics. The [[Cult of the Dragon]] and their prophet, who wielded a talon created by [[Takhisis]] herself, the [[Sword of Sorrow]].

After the skies broke open, the [[Astral Plane-Sea]] itself splitting into the [[Material Plane]], mortals watched gods in their colossal spiralling eldritch forms, cutting each other down.

After [[Takhisis]] had shown her hand, her allies, and her plans, [[AO]] ([[Mystra]]) appeared above [[Nyropa]] in a semi-comprehendible avatar, killing multiples gods ([[Atropal]], [[Bane]], [[Bhaal]], [[Thrym]], & [[Maglubiyet]]). [[AO]], all powerful as they are, punished [[Takhisis]] to endure life from the very beginning of time, all over again - having stripped her of everything she fought for, everything she did... all of it, yet to happen. This cosmic reset, and damning punishment, weakened [[AO]]/[[Mystra]] immensely, and thus to protect themselves, entombed themselves in the [[Material Plane]], within a new island, [[The Isle of Black - The Mystery Within|The Isle of Black]]. Thus bringing the world, and all its realms to peace.

[[AO]]'s & [[Mystra]]'s name has been expunged from the record ever since. Scriptures magically rewritten, names omitted from history itself - written only that Celestials may read it ([[Oblivati Mori ]](To Die Forgotten) = Law of Theothinatic Silence - when a god dies, mortals forget their name). And with them, the secrets of the [[Weave]], leaving the world feeling less exuberant, and mysterious since their short visit.

This event was called [[The Reckoning]], and now to referred to as [[The Great Forgotten War]]
