Shar's followers were directed to "quench the light of the moon" by stealing gifts meant for Selûne and defeating her faithful at every opportunity. They were to never hide from the light and always take action while under the protection of darkness. According to some sects of the faith, she desired that they destroy all the deities of the [multiverse](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Multiverse "Multiverse"), and undo existence itself.

The doctrine of the Sharran church placed no restrictions on crimes committed for personal gain, including but not limited to: theft, smuggling, kidnapping, murder, or underhanded business practices. Quote often though, most of Shar's followers served not for personal enrichment, but promise of her "Dark Reward": immortality by means of transition into [undeath](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Undeath "Undeath").

### Tenets[](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Church_of_Shar?veaction=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Tenets")

Shar's faithful were taught to transcend fear and loss suffered by members of the weaker faiths.[[14]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Church_of_Shar#cite_note-BGIII-15) Their most valuable commodity was their secrecy. Information was closely guarded and only revealed when it was absolutely essential, or traded for similarly confidential information.[[18]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Church_of_Shar#cite_note-LoD-p123-19) Each member of the church maintained their ["Own Secret"](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Church_of_Shar#Admittance) with Shar, pertaining to their proof of devotion to her service.[[17]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Church_of_Shar#cite_note-N.WoN-p55-18)

Clergy members followed Shar's 'Thirteen Truths', foundational beliefs that formed the core of their faith. Among these were:[[19]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Church_of_Shar#cite_note-Shadowstorm-20)

- Love is a lie. Only hate endures.
- Light is blinding. Only in darkness do we see clearly.

Hidden with a [holy Sharran tome](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/The_Leaves_of_One_Night "The Leaves of One Night") was Shar's Fourteenth Truth:[[20]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Church_of_Shar#cite_note-Godborn-p278-21)

- All is meaningless. And nothing endures.
