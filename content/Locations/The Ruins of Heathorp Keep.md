### Founding -  Years Ago
Surrounded by mostly evil/dangerous locations, this location was founded under the intention of protecting all those that are good, from those that are evil, or would seek them harm. Originally known as [[The Ruins of Heathorp Keep|Heathorp Keep]], this town and enclosed castle was the resting place of many wayward travellers and asylum seekers, in addition to heroic figures of statue.
