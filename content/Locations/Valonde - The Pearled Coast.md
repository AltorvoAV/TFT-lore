# Valonde
## Basic Info
- Government: [[Plutocracy]]
- Leaders: [[The Council of Valonde]]
- Alignment: Lawful

### Founding - 607 BR
Valonde was founded by a group of traders, ~600 years ago, who formed a [[Plutocracy]].
The ruling [[The Council of Valonde|Council of Valonde]], is made from the 5 most wealthy people in Valonde. As such, rulers have come and gone over the years.
The city has always seen magic as a commodity/strength - had large trades and friendship with [[Porthca - The Arcane Bridge|Porthca]] & [[Brugrove - City of the Iron Guard|Brugrove]] for many reasons.
