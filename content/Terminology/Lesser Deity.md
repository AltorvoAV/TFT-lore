Among the [[Entities/Gods/Gods|Deities]] worshiped by mortals, a [[Lesser Deity]] was of middling power when ordered by rank.

According to many clergies, they generally had thousands or millions of followers and commanded some respect from their divine peers. Other [[Lesser Deity]] had millions or billions of followers, but lacked divine power to ascend in rank, amongst their peers; for example, [[Yondalla]].
