## Events:
[[The Ruins of Heathorp Keep|Heathorp Keep]] was a prosperous sanctuary for all. It stood to challenge darkness of both body and mind. Its citizens rejoiced in its peace and tranquillity. Heroes of many gods and nations found their home here, ensuring safety and kindness for all.

In their blind acceptance, many [[Shar|Sharran]]s managed to infiltrate the Keep, and make it their 'home'.

[[Shar]] waited patiently for their numbers to grow, and strength to accumulate. On the first day of celebration to their great goddess [[Selûne]] in the year 168AR, [[Shar]] gave her orders to vanquish the city and drown it in darkness.

Her chosen [[Shadow Adept]]; [[Quar Rhodon]] & [[Aldric Vane]].
- [[Quar Rhodon|Quar]] chosen for his past connection with [[Selûne]]'s church and gifted artefact empowered by the god of magic: the [[Astral Sextant]].
- [[Aldric Vane|Aldric]] chosen for his ability to surgically remove light from [[Nyropa]], and knowledge gifted to him by [[Shar]] & [[Oghma]] - knowledge required to complete the [[Well of Damnation]] ritual.

With her chosen, sworn; and the cities' guard down - they struck.

None stood in their way. They massacred [[South Docks]], and stormed the [[Cathedral of Moonlight]], killing all that defended it, including 2 celestials: Deva & Solar.

Once the ritual was complete and [[Quar Rhodon|Quar]]'s greataxe swung decapitation the Solar, [[Selûne]]'s blessings were swallowed whole, light eradicated, and all [[The Weave|Weave]] energy was sucked into the well, making the entire surrounding area a massive [[Dead Zones|Dead Zone]].
