## Events:
[[Scholar's Farthing]] was a utopia... UNTIL THE SKAPA HIT.

45 kids and their teacher were sent into a deep spiral. Their teacher committed suicide by fire ([[Lil Cindi]]'s fav) and the kids went to the funeral where they said words and sparks were lit in the damp.

[[Captain Joblin Mathews]] was tasked with turning this children into soldiers. And with 4 rounds of extraneous training, soldiers emerged from the [[Greater Lion's District]], into the [[Sea Broth]].

4 days at sea; these hardly warriors determined who would be in the vanguard to storm the shores of [[Porthca - The Arcane Bridge|Porthca]]. Hearing [[Captain Joblin Mathews|Captain Joblin]]'s plan to 'catch the Porthcans "off guard" by attacking the Eastern front', each former drop-out thought themselves ready and prepared for whatever was coming...

They simply were not.
