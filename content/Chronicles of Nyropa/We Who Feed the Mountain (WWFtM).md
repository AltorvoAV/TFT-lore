## Events:
**The [[Shazahath Mountains - The Ruined Caverns|Shazahath Mountains]] — a sprawling, ancient range riddled with caverns — have gone silent.**

Our tale begins fifty years before the events of [[index|The Forgotten Tomb]], in a time when heroes still shaped the wild lands through steel, spell, and legend.

You are a well-known adventuring company ([[The Sunrise Establishment (WWFtM)]]), the kind whose name carries weight in taverns and courts alike. Your deeds are known from coast to crown — and it’s those very deeds that have drawn the eye of [[King Eldrin]], ruler of [[Brugrove - City of the Iron Guard|Brugrove]].

The king has summoned you for a new task.

A small party was sent north, into the [[Shazahath Mountains - The Ruined Caverns|Shazahath Mountains]], answering a strange plea for help — a group of kobolds who claimed to serve something they called _“[[Korraeth, Voice of the Flame|The Great Flame]].”_

Weeks have passed.
No word has come back.

Now the king turns to you. He asks that you travel to a remote mountain village near the kobolds’ territory, discover what became of the first party, and uncover the truth behind these whispers of the “Great Flame.”
